================================================================================
COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How long does a full planning engagement take?
A: 2-4 hours total (Phase 1: 30-60 min, Phase 2: 45-90 min, Phase 3: 60-120 min)

Q: Can I skip Phase 2?
A: No. Phase 2 is your last chance to review architecture before detailed 
   specification. Skipping it causes alignment problems later.

Q: What if I want different technologies?
A: Perfect use of Phase 2. Request revisions before proceeding to Phase 3.
   Phase 2 is designed for iteration until stakeholder alignment.

Q: Can this work for small projects?
A: Yes. Adjust documentation depth appropriately. A 3-person startup SDD 
   is simpler than enterprise, but follows the same framework.

Q: What if requirements change during implementation?
A: Document the reason in the SDD. Update the document as architecture 
   evolves. Major changes should trigger Phase 2 revision.

Q: Is this suitable for legacy system modernization?
A: Yes, excellent for planning migrations. Helps systematically decompose 
   monoliths and plan incremental modernization.

Q: Can we use different technologies than those in catalogs?
A: The catalogs are bounded references, not restrictions. New technologies 
   can be added if well-justified. Update catalogs annually.

Q: Who needs to approve at each gate?
A: Product owner, tech lead, or stakeholder representing final decision-making. 
   Must be explicit approval, not passive acceptance.
